About Me

Hi, I'm Taiga. I'm a Sophomore in Purdue's College of Engineering.

I started my journey at Purdue in Robotics Engineering Technology in the Polytechnic Institute, but soon realized that Mechanical Engineering was a better fit for me.

My interests span robotics (as one might expect), aerospace, electronics (particularly microcontroller development), and technical theatre production. What drives me most is the pursuit of optimization and efficiency—finding elegant solutions to complex problems.

I love optimization, efficiency, and improvement, but even more importantly the ability to communicate and work in a team.
